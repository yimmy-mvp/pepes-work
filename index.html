See Like a Bee

Zum Goldenen Hirschen

Challenge: Bees see flowers differently. How can OBI help plant what matters?

Strategy: On World Bee Day, we created an Instagram filter simulating the bee color spectrum, helping gardeners plant smarter.

Result: Over 110K flower buyers turned to OBI’s filter to check for bee-friendly flowers.

Don’t speak unless you can improve the silence.

Rediscover Germany

Ogilvy Frankfurt

Challenge: Summer 2020. A pandemic shuts down global travel and the industry goes silent. Millions canceled plans.

Strategy: Instead aof exotic destinations, we reminded travelers that they could get the same experiences without leaving Germany.

Result: Deutsche Bahn shot up +10 points in brand popularity during travel bans.

Actions speak louder than words.

People believe what they see, not what they’re told.
