Tested by the Internet

Challenge: Bosch says its tools are tough. The internet doesn’t care.

Strategy: We handed over testing to real people—letting Instagram votes decide how Bosch tools got tested.

Result: 50K+ votes turned into extreme tests, reaching new audiences Bosch never had before.

Rediscover Germany

Challenge: Summer 2020. A pandemic shut down global travel. Millions canceled plans.

Strategy: Instead of promoting new destinations, we reminded travelers that they could get the same experiences without leaving Germany.

Result: Deutsche Bahn shot up +10 points in brand popularity during travel bans.

See Like a Bee

Challenge: Bees see flowers differently than humans. How do we help people plant what really matters?

Strategy: On World Bee Day, we created an Instagram filter simulating the bee color spectrum, helping gardeners plant smarter.

Result: Over 110K flower buyers turned to OBI’s filter to check for bee-friendly flowers.
