See Like a Bee

Challenge

Bees see flowers differently than humans. How can we help gardeners plant the most attractive flowers?

Strategy

On World Bee Day, we created an Instagram filter simulating the color spectrum visible to bees. Gardeners could switch it on to identify the most attractive colors and help bees find their way to their gardens.

Result

More than 110K flower buyers used the filter to identify the best colors for bees.
