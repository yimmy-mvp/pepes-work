Why Hire Pepe Sánchez?

Three works that prove it.

See Like a Bee

Challenge: Bees see flowers differently than humans. How can we help gardeners plant the most attractive flowers?

Strategy: On World Bee Day, we created an Instagram filter simulating the bee color spectrum, helping gardeners attract bees.

Result: Over 110K flower buyers used it.

Rediscover Germany

Challenge: Summer 2020. A pandemic disrupts global travel. Millions cancel plans.

Strategy: We reminded travelers that they could stay in Germany and get similar experiences to those international ones, just a train ride away.

Result: Deutsche Bahn got the top place with +10 points on the most popular brands.

Tested by the Internet

Challenge: Bosch is Invented for Life, not for labs. How do we prove it?

Strategy: We let the internet vote on how to test Bosch tools, using Instagram polls.

Result: More than 50K votes turned into challenges, reaching untapped Bosch audiences.
